Approach

A thoughtful framework for student-centered change.

The approach page explains how MapFixStudents listens, maps needs, aligns resources, and keeps students involved at every step.

Listen carefully

Gather lived experience from students, educators, and families to understand barriers before proposing solutions.

Map the gaps

Use clear information design to show where support exists, where it is missing, and where coordination can improve access.

Build partnerships

Connect schools, nonprofits, and local leaders around shared priorities that move from ideas into action.

Measure and refine

Track outcomes, learn quickly, and keep improving the system so it stays responsive to student needs.

Clear process creates lasting support.
